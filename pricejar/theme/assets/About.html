How It Works

Steps to follow in order to navigate through our website





Create an account /Sign In


Create an account or Sign In to your existing
account to have access to all the wonderful
features our platform has ranging from item
search and comparing of items with other stores.



Search for an Item


Once you are done creating an account or signing
to your existing account, browse through the list
of items or input an item name on the search bar
or filter your search to narrow down your search



Get redirected to the store


Once you have gotten the item you want, you can compare the prices with other store and you will be redirected to the store for a proper review of the item on the store and make your purchase of the item
