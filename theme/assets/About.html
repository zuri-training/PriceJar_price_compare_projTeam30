How It Works

Steps to follow in order to navigate through our website




Create Account/Sign in

Create an account or Sign In to your existing account to have access to all the wonderful features our platform has ranging from item search and comparing of items with other stores



Browse for your item

Once you are done creating an account or signing to your existing account, browse through the list of items or input an item name on the search bar or filter your search to narrow down your search



Browse for your item

Once you have gotten the item you want, you can compare the prices with other store and you will be redirected to the store for a proper review of the item on the store and make your purchase of the item
